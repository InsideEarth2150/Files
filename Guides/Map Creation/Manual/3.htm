1. We recommend that you set your starting points near veins of mineral resources. It can be pretty annoying if you have to look all over the place for your starting points later!

  2. You have to keep in mind just how fast your computer is when you're adding additional items like   trees, bushes, rocks, etc. The more of these you add, the slower the game will be.
  3. Save the game area often. This way you won't lose any of your changes.
  4. Remember to leave enough room for construction of your base.
  5. Just as in real life, don't build your structures on sand or cliffs!
